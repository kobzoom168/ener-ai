Privacy Policy

Service: Ener Auto Post  ·  Website: https://my-ener.uk/  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Ener Auto Post (“we”, “our”, the “Service”) is an in-house content tool we use to create short videos and publish them to our own social media accounts, including TikTok. This page explains what data we handle when the Service connects to TikTok.

Information we collect

  • TikTok account basic info (such as your account display name) via the user.info.basic scope, used only to confirm which account is connected.
  • Authorization tokens (access token and refresh token) that TikTok issues after you grant access. These are required to publish videos on your behalf.

We do not collect your TikTok password, your followers' data, your private messages, or any personal data of other users.

How we use it

  • To upload and publish videos that we produce to the connected TikTok account (the video.publish / video.upload scopes).
  • To show the connected account name so the operator can confirm the correct account.

We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

Storage & security

Tokens are stored on our own private server and are used only to call the official TikTok API over HTTPS. Access is limited to the account owner.

Data retention & deletion

Tokens are kept only while the Service is connected. You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings (Security & login → Manage app permissions), which immediately stops our ability to post. To request deletion of stored tokens, contact us at the email below.
